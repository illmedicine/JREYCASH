Privacy & Confidentiality Agreement

THIS AGREEMENT is entered into on this date by and between Jrey Cash (hereinafter "Disclosing Party") and You (hereinafter "Receiving Party").

WHEREAS, the Disclosing Party is a public figure who highly values their personal privacy and the privacy of their guests;

WHEREAS, the Receiving Party has been invited to a private location or event (the "Meeting") with the Disclosing Party;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises herein, the parties agree as follows:

Section 1 of 7

Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" shall include, but is not limited to, any and all non-public information relating to the Disclosing Party, their family, friends, associates, or other guests. This includes:

  • The occurrence, location, and time of the Meeting.
  • The identity of the Disclosing Party and any other individuals present.
  • Any conversations, behaviors, personal habits, or activities observed or participated in during the Meeting.
  • The physical layout, security measures, or location details of the private premises.

Section 2 of 7

Non-Disclosure & Non-Disparagement

The Receiving Party agrees to maintain the strictest confidentiality regarding all Confidential Information. The Receiving Party shall not:

  • Discuss, publish, post, or disseminate any Confidential Information to any third party, including family members, friends, journalists, or media outlets.
  • Post any details, cryptic or explicit, on any social media platforms, blogs, or public forums.
  • Make any derogatory, disparaging, or negative statements regarding the Disclosing Party that could harm their public image, reputation, or business interests.

Section 3 of 7

Prohibition of Electronic Devices & Recording

To ensure the absolute privacy of all parties, the Receiving Party agrees to the following restrictions regarding electronic devices:

  • Surrender of Devices: The Receiving Party agrees to surrender all mobile phones, smartwatches, cameras, recording devices, or any other electronic transmission devices upon entry to the premises, to be securely stored and returned upon departure.
  • No Unauthorized Recording: Under no circumstances shall the Receiving Party photograph, film, audio-record, or otherwise capture any media of the Disclosing Party, the premises, or the Meeting.

Section 4 of 7

Protected Disclosures (Legal Exceptions)

Nothing in this Agreement is intended to or shall be construed to prevent, impede, or interfere with the Receiving Party's right to:

  • Report any suspected criminal activity or illegal acts to appropriate law enforcement agencies.
  • Participate in any investigation conducted by a local, state, or federal government agency.
  • Testify truthfully under oath pursuant to a valid subpoena or court order.

Any clause in this Agreement that would otherwise restrict these legal rights is void and unenforceable.

Section 5 of 7

Return of Property

Upon request by the Disclosing Party, or immediately upon the conclusion of the Meeting, the Receiving Party agrees to return or destroy any physical or digital materials in their possession that contain Confidential Information.

Section 6 of 7

Remedies & Liquidated Damages

The Receiving Party acknowledges that a breach of this Agreement will cause irreparable harm to the Disclosing Party's reputation, brand, and privacy, the exact financial impact of which would be difficult to calculate.

  • In the event of a breach, the Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek immediate injunctive relief to prevent further disclosure.
  • The Receiving Party agrees to be held liable for liquidated damages for each unauthorized disclosure, in addition to any legal fees incurred by the Disclosing Party in enforcing this Agreement.

Section 7 of 7

Severability & Execution

If any provision of this Agreement is found to be void, invalid, or unenforceable by a court of competent jurisdiction, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of this date.
